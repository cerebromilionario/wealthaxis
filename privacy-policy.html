Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

WealthAxis ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at wealthaxis.netlify.app. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information collected from visitors to our website.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect (Including Minimal Data)

We collect only the information needed to operate, secure, and improve WealthAxis. Depending on how you use the site, this may include:

  • Information you provide voluntarily: If you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we collect the details you submit (such as your email address and message content).
  • Automatically collected technical data: IP address, approximate location (based on IP), browser type, device type, operating system, referral source, pages viewed, interaction events, and visit timestamps.
  • Advertising and cookie identifiers: Cookie IDs, advertising IDs, and similar identifiers used by us and advertising partners (including Google AdSense/Google Ads) to measure ad performance and deliver personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Consent and preference data: Your cookie and privacy preferences, where required by applicable law.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, secure, and improve the website and its content.
  • Send newsletters and updates only when you have subscribed.
  • Understand site usage, performance, and engagement trends.
  • Display ads, limit ad fraud, measure campaign effectiveness, and support personalized advertising where permitted.
  • Comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Use, and protect our users and services.
  • Respond to questions, requests, and support inquiries.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given us explicit consent (e.g., newsletter subscription).
  • Legitimate interests: For analytics, website improvement, and fraud prevention.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by applicable law.

5. Third Parties and Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers and partners that help us operate WealthAxis, deliver analytics, and provide advertising.

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and audience insights.
  • Google AdSense / Google Ads: Ad delivery, ad measurement, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and personalized or contextual advertising.
  • Email service providers: Newsletter distribution and subscription management.
  • Netlify and infrastructure providers: Hosting, security, and website performance.

These providers may process identifiers, technical data, and usage data according to their own privacy terms and our instructions where applicable. We may also disclose data when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

6. Cookies, Tracking Technologies, and Personalized Ads

We use cookies, pixels, local storage, tags, and similar technologies ("cookies" for short) to run the website, understand usage, and support advertising. Some cookies are set by us, and others are set by third parties such as Google.

Categories of cookies and tracking technologies we use include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand interactions, traffic sources, and performance (for example, Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense/Google Ads and partners to show personalized or non-personalized ads, measure ad performance, and reduce repeated ad delivery.
  • Preference cookies: Remember settings such as language, consent, and user choices.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You can also manage ad personalization in Google Ads Settings and find broader industry opt-out tools at aboutads.info/choices. Disabling some cookies may impact site functionality.

7. Google AdSense / Google Ads Notice

We use Google AdSense and related Google advertising services. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to collect and process data for ad personalization, ad delivery, measurement, reporting, and fraud prevention.

Google may use data from your visits to this and other websites/apps to personalize ads. To learn more, please review Google's page: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

For Google's broader privacy practices, see Google Privacy Policy.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time, and their data will be removed from our mailing list promptly.

9. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR/LGPD and Similar Laws)

Depending on your location, including the EEA/UK (GDPR) and Brazil (LGPD), you may have rights related to your personal data:

  • Access and confirmation: Ask whether we process your data and request a copy.
  • Correction: Request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
  • Deletion/anonymization: Request deletion or anonymization when applicable.
  • Objection or restriction: Object to certain processing activities or request restriction.
  • Portability: Request data portability where legally applicable.
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke consent at any time for consent-based processing.
  • Information about sharing: Request information about public/private entities with which data is shared (LGPD context).
  • Review of automated decisions: Request review where decisions are based solely on automated processing, when applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority or data protection authority.

10. Children's Privacy

WealthAxis is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please visit our Contact page.
