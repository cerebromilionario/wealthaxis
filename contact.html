Frequently Asked Questions

Is WealthAxis free to use?

Yes, completely free. All articles, guides, and investment calculators are available at no cost. We're supported by advertising revenue through Google AdSense.

Is the content on WealthAxis financial advice?

No. All content is for educational and informational purposes only. Please read our full Disclaimer and consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.

How do I unsubscribe from the newsletter?

Every newsletter email includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Click it to be immediately removed from our mailing list. You can also contact us directly to request removal.

Can I republish or share WealthAxis content?

You may share links to our articles freely. However, reproducing or republishing our full content without written permission is not permitted. Contact us for licensing inquiries.

How accurate are the investment calculators?

Our calculators use standard financial formulas and are designed for educational illustration. They assume constant rates of return and do not account for taxes, fees, or market volatility. Results should not be used as the sole basis for financial decisions.

How can I advertise on WealthAxis?

We run Google AdSense for programmatic advertising. For direct sponsorship or branded content opportunities, please use the contact form above and select "Advertising & Partnerships."
