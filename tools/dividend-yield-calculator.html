Dividend Yield Calculator: FAQ

What is a good dividend yield?

For US equities, 2% to 4% is often considered healthy and sustainable. Very high yields can indicate risk.

Why yield on cost matters?

Yield on cost shows your income return relative to your buy price, useful for long-term dividend growth investors.
